Adopt a shelter dog!

At PAWs, we make sure that everyone gets a happy home! Not to worry, our animals are very well taken care of and we at PAWs ensure that future dog adopters would get dogs that have been in the best health and already been neutered. Here at PAWs, we encourage you to adopt a very loving dog that suits you best!

Here's how to apply:

  • Send an application form
  • Wait for contacts from our Tech team
  • Submit everything needed for adoption
  • Paying of the adoption fee
  • Zoom meetings with the dogs!
  • Select your forever buddy
  • Adopt!

Frequently Asked Questions



How can I adopt from PAWS?

In order to adopt, there will be a submission form of basic information that will be verified upon on
the following Zoom Meetings once the techs have confirmed the submission.

Can you adopt my pet?

Yes! We can. Being a fur-parent is not an easy task and giving them new homes is our job. With the submission form, we are able to contact you on the details and the process on how will we adopt your furbaby.

Why is there an adoption fee?


The adoption fee will only go to our lovely dogs! We do not benefit from the adoption fee
and it'll all be used for every needs of our lovely pets.

Can I adopt more than one pet?

Yes, you may certainly do, although there will be qualifications that will be verified in the Zoom meeting
in order to ensure that you are more than qualified to adopt more than one pet.

Do you have purebred cats or dogs?

We do, although it is a very rare occassion that we get purebred animals.
Since we mainly focus on rescuing the ones that have been abused or abandoned, we do not look at their breeds but rather the
situation that they have been on.
