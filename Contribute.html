VOLUNTEER TODAY


As a volunteer for PAWS, you will be assigned tasks such as dog walking, kennel cleaning, and assisting during outreach programs. The organization welcomes volunteers with full-time jobs and families, and encourages individuals to use their unique skills and interests to contribute to the cause.




What is required to become a volunteer?


All volunteers must attend orientation, which will be announced on social media. Follow the organization's social media pages to stay informed.




Is there a strict schedule or time quota for volunteer work?


Volunteers can choose to volunteer once a week, once a month, or whenever their schedule allows. Shelter volunteer work is available within operating hours, Monday-Saturday except Wednesdays and holidays, between 8am to 5pm.




Is there an age requirement for volunteers?


Minors must attend the orientation with a parent or guardian. Children below 13 must be supervised by their parent or guardian when volunteering.




What is an "active volunteer"?


Active volunteers are those who render at least 8 hours a month doing shelter or out-of-shelter work. They get included in special activities, events and workshops. Volunteers who foster shelter animals are automatically credited with 8 hours per month.




I can't make it to orientation. Can I still volunteer?


All volunteers are required to attend orientation as a certain level of knowledge and familiarity with the organization's policies is required to perform volunteer duties.




Can I get a certificate for my volunteer work?


The organization is a charity, and does not provide volunteer certificates for academic compliance. Volunteering is viewed as an altruistic activity with no expectation of financial or social gain.




Can I submit my application form on the day of the orientation?


All applications must be submitted within the designated period to allow time for screening and confirmation of attendees before the scheduled orientation date.
